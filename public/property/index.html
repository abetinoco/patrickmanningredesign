About This Property

Experience modern country living on 6.62 private acres in the highly desirable Berlin community of Lewisburg, TN. Built in 2021, this custom 3-bedroom, 3-bath home offers approximately 2,400 square feet of thoughtfully designed living space combined with rare functional improvements that are increasingly difficult to find in today's market.

Inside, you'll find an open-concept layout with spacious living areas, a well-appointed kitchen ideal for entertaining, and a generous primary suite designed for comfort and privacy. The newer construction provides energy efficiency, low maintenance, and peace of mind.

What truly sets this property apart is the unmatched versatility of the improvements:

  • 28×40 insulated shop — ideal for business, hobbies, equipment, or storage
  • 19×11 heated and cooled office with additional storage space
  • 42×20 barn with add-ons
  • 16×12 metal chicken coop
  • 16×11 separate heated + cooled cabin with solar power and deck — perfect for guests, studio, or recreational retreat
  • Attached garage and carport

This property offers flexibility for multi-generational living, business-ready, remote work, homesteading, hobby farming, or recreational use. With over 6 acres of usable land, there's space for animals, gardens, additional structures, or simply room to breathe.

Located in the desirable Duck River / Columbia corridor of Marshall County, the property benefits from long-term recreational and land appeal in one of Middle Tennessee's most attractive rural growth areas.

If you've been searching for a newer home with acreage, workshop space, guest cabin, and privacy — all within reach of Lewisburg and Columbia — this is a rare opportunity.
